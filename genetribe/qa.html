Questions and Answers

Q: What genomes of species is A suitable for?

A: GeneTribe takes advantages of colinear block scores and chromosome sets information, and it is suitable for homologues inference among complex polyploids that are genetically closed assemblies, such as wheat, cotton, etc.

Q: Can I run GeneTribe based on pre-computed BLAST files?

A: Yes. GeneTribe can running based on pre-computed files.
1. Move files to directory DIR1.
2. Add the parameter -d DIR1 to command genetribe core.
